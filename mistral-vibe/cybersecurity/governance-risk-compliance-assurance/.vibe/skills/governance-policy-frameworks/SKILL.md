# Governance Policy Frameworks

Use this procedure to prepare governance and framework artifacts that are traceable, scoped, and ready for human review.

1. Confirm scope, audience, source authorities, applicable entities, framework version, and human approval owner.
2. Map requirements to controls and label each mapping as direct, inferred, not applicable, or blocked.
3. Draft governance cadence, RACI notes, control-owner matrix, and gap register.
4. Record decisions, assumptions, unavailable evidence, and external-claim restrictions.
5. Request `independent-assurance-reviewer` before completion.

Stop for legal interpretation, unsupported compliance claims, missing authoritative sources, customer-facing claims, regulator-facing claims, or approval requests.
