# Assurance Third Party Reporting

Use this procedure to prepare assurance and reporting outputs that make evidence status, gaps, and ownership visible.

1. Confirm audience, reporting period, control/vendor/maturity scope, evidence sources, and approval owner.
2. Map controls, vendors, metrics, or maturity criteria to supplied evidence.
3. Classify evidence as supplied, missing, stale, inferred, or not applicable.
4. Prepare evidence requests, vendor or maturity summaries, remediation status, and escalation notes.
5. Request `independent-assurance-reviewer` before completion.

Stop for audit sign-off, vendor approval, compliance certification, unsupported maturity claims, or external distribution.
