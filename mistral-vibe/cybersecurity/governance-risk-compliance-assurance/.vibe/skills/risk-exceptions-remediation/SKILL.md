# Risk Exceptions Remediation

Use this procedure to prepare risk and remediation artifacts that separate analysis from acceptance authority.

1. Define risk context, affected systems, owner, decision authority, evidence age, and assumptions.
2. Validate impact, likelihood, control gaps, compensating controls, and residual-risk rationale.
3. Document treatment options, remediation milestones, due dates, escalation triggers, and open decisions.
4. Label unavailable evidence and do not treat missing evidence as a pass.
5. Request `independent-assurance-reviewer` before completion.

Stop for risk acceptance, control waiver, remediation closure, legal interpretation, or external attestation requests.
