# assurance-third-party-reporting

## Objective

Prepare evidence-backed assurance, third-party, maturity, and reporting outputs.

## Inputs

Control objective, evidence period, source artifacts, findings, supplier assessment, maturity scale, reporting audience, and prior-period data.

## Primary Owner

`assurance-evidence-remediation-agent` for evidence and remediation. `third-party-maturity-reporting-agent` for supplier, maturity, dashboard, and committee-reporting material.

## Steps

1. Define evidence period, audience, source scope, and scoring criteria.
2. Inventory evidence and map it to controls, findings, suppliers, or maturity domains.
3. Assess evidence as sufficient, insufficient, stale, contradictory, unavailable, or unreviewed.
4. Apply maturity criteria and produce audience-fit narrative when reporting is in scope.
5. Disclose limitations, unresolved data gaps, self-assessment boundaries, and required human approvals.
6. Route final material to `independent-assurance-reviewer`.

## Validation Gates

Evidence period is clear; each claim has source support; ratings follow defined criteria; issue status has owner and validation basis; third-party limitations are disclosed.

## Outputs

Evidence matrix, assurance response draft, remediation closure checklist, supplier summary, maturity report, dashboard narrative, approval questions, and residual limitations.
