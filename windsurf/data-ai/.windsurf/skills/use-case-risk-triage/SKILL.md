# Use-Case Risk Triage

## Use When

- New Data and AI request.
- Ambiguous ownership, risk, feasibility, or value.
- Portfolio intake, impact classification, or routing.

## Procedure

1. Capture outcome, users, decision impact, affected data, system boundary, proposed AI role, alternatives to AI, adoption goal, success metric, and lifecycle owner.
2. Classify sensitivity, lawful/permitted use, high-impact status, human oversight need, third-party dependency, model/provider dependency, and operational criticality.
3. Identify missing inputs, assumptions, constraints, approval owners, evidence needs, and stop conditions.
4. Route to the minimum necessary specialist Skills or Workflows while preserving independent final assurance.
5. Return `PASS`, `FAIL`, or `BLOCKED` with exact missing evidence and next owner.
