# Data and AI Human Review Gate

Apply this rule when Data and AI work affects high-impact decisions, production data, provider/model selection, policy exceptions, risk acceptance, publishing, deployment, signing, spending, external integrations, destructive actions, credentials, access expansion, or irreversible outcomes.

Require explicit human review before proceeding. The decision package must include owner, scope, data/model/provider versions, provenance, permitted-use evidence, expected benefit, alternatives to AI, impacted users, risk controls, monitoring, rollback or retirement path, unresolved risks, and completion status as `PASS`, `FAIL`, or `BLOCKED`.
