# Rectification, Retirement, Evidence Retention, and Final Assurance

1. Confirm affected records, datasets, embeddings, indexes, model artifacts, prompts, caches, metrics, dashboards, logs, downstream systems, and owners.
2. Use `ops-risk-assurance` to verify deletion/rectification propagation, retention evidence, rollback or retirement readiness, unresolved incidents, risk acceptance, and final traceability.
3. Use specialist Skills for any missing data, analytics, ML, GenAI, or platform evidence before final assurance.
4. Final assurance must be independent from the builders and the coordinator.
5. Return `PASS`, `FAIL`, or `BLOCKED` with remaining limitations and human decision points.
