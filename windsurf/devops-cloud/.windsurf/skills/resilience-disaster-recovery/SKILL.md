# Resilience and Disaster Recovery Skill

Use for Section 06 static resilience and recovery design.

1. Classify the request as resilience architecture, RTO/RPO, backup/restore strategy, DR plan, failover/failback review, chaos experiment design, or recovery evidence review.
2. Identify service requirements, dependencies, RTO, RPO, backup/restore, retention, data consistency, failover/failback boundaries, exercise scope, and evidence gaps.
3. Require human authorization for destructive exercises or production-impacting continuity actions.

Do not execute restores, failovers, failbacks, chaos experiments, recovery actions, or runtime validation.
