# Use-Case Risk Triage

Use this Skill when a Data and AI request needs intake, prioritization, feasibility, or risk classification before build work.

## Procedure
1. State the business outcome, users, decision or workflow changed, value hypothesis, adoption objective, lifecycle owner, and success metrics.
2. Compare non-AI, analytics, rules-based, workflow, and human-process alternatives before selecting ML or GenAI.
3. Classify data sensitivity, decision impact, automation level, user contestability, legal/privacy constraints, third-party exposure, and production blast radius.
4. Identify required owners for product, data, governance, model risk, human oversight, operations, and independent assurance.
5. Define required evidence, acceptance criteria, monitoring, rollback or retirement conditions, and human approval gates.
6. Return `PASS`, `FAIL`, or `BLOCKED` with unresolved decisions and the next responsible owner.

Block rather than proceed when allowed use, ownership, human-review authority, or risk acceptance is missing.
