# Governance, Policy, and Frameworks

Objective: create decision-ready governance, policy, control, and compliance mapping artifacts.

Primary owner: `governance-policy-frameworks-agent`.

Workflow:

1. Confirm scope, exclusions, audience, decision rights, owner, reviewer, approver, and evidence period.
2. Inventory applicable governance artifacts, policies, standards, controls, requirements, and evidence.
3. Draft or review without changing source meaning.
4. Classify mappings as exact, partial, inherited, compensating, non-applicable, or unmapped.
5. Identify gaps, conflicts, dependencies, residual risk, and human-only decisions.
6. Route high-impact outputs to `independent-assurance-reviewer`.

Outputs: charter, RACI, policy draft or review, control entry, mapping matrix, gap record, change-impact assessment, decision package, assumptions, limitations, and approval state.

Stop conditions: strategy approval, policy publication, legal applicability decision, compliance or certification claim, control enforcement, or unsupported source material.
