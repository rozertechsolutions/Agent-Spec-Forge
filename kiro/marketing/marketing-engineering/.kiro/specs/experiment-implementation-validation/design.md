# Experiment Implementation and Validation — Design

## Sequence
1. Freeze experiment contract
2. Implement assignment, flags, and exposure
3. Validate balance, persistence, contamination, metrics, and failure behavior
4. Configure monitoring and kill switch
5. Independent QA
6. Prepare launch/stop approval

## Gates
- Intake
- Evidence or technical baseline
- Compliance/security
- Independent quality
- Human decision
