# Experiment Implementation and Validation — Requirements

## Objective
Execute `experiment-implementation-validation` within the branch governance and prepare a human decision package.

## Requirements
1. The workflow shall complete the `Freeze experiment contract` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
2. The workflow shall complete the `Implement assignment, flags, and exposure` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
3. The workflow shall complete the `Validate balance, persistence, contamination, metrics, and failure behavior` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
4. The workflow shall complete the `Configure monitoring and kill switch` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
5. The workflow shall complete the `Independent QA` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
6. The workflow shall complete the `Prepare launch/stop approval` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.

## Cross-cutting acceptance
- No external or production action occurs.
- Independent review is completed.
- Blockers stop progression.
- Final authority remains human.
