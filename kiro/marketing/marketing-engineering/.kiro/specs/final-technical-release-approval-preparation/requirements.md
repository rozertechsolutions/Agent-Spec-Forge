# Final Technical Release Approval Preparation — Requirements

## Objective
Execute `final-technical-release-approval-preparation` within the branch governance and prepare a human decision package.

## Requirements
1. The workflow shall complete the `Trace requirements to implementation and tests` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
2. The workflow shall complete the `Verify security, privacy, accessibility, and performance` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
3. The workflow shall complete the `Confirm observability and rollback` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
4. The workflow shall complete the `Reconcile findings and scope changes` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
5. The workflow shall complete the `Verify no secrets or production actions occurred` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
6. The workflow shall complete the `Set readiness state and requested human decision` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.

## Cross-cutting acceptance
- No external or production action occurs.
- Independent review is completed.
- Blockers stop progression.
- Final authority remains human.
