# Final Technical Release Approval Preparation — Design

## Sequence
1. Trace requirements to implementation and tests
2. Verify security, privacy, accessibility, and performance
3. Confirm observability and rollback
4. Reconcile findings and scope changes
5. Verify no secrets or production actions occurred
6. Set readiness state and requested human decision

## Gates
- Intake
- Evidence or technical baseline
- Compliance/security
- Independent quality
- Human decision
