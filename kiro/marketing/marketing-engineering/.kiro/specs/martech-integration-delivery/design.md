# Martech Integration Delivery — Design

## Sequence
1. Validate purpose and owners
2. Define contracts, identities, preferences, deletion, and scopes
3. Implement or specify idempotency, retries, and observability
4. Test mapping, failures, replay, opt-out, and deletion
5. Security/privacy review
6. Runbook and rollback
7. Prepare activation approval

## Gates
- Intake
- Evidence or technical baseline
- Compliance/security
- Independent quality
- Human decision
