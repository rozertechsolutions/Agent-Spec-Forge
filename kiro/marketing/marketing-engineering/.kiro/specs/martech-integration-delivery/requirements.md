# Martech Integration Delivery — Requirements

## Objective
Execute `martech-integration-delivery` within the branch governance and prepare a human decision package.

## Requirements
1. The workflow shall complete the `Validate purpose and owners` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
2. The workflow shall complete the `Define contracts, identities, preferences, deletion, and scopes` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
3. The workflow shall complete the `Implement or specify idempotency, retries, and observability` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
4. The workflow shall complete the `Test mapping, failures, replay, opt-out, and deletion` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
5. The workflow shall complete the `Security/privacy review` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
6. The workflow shall complete the `Runbook and rollback` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
7. The workflow shall complete the `Prepare activation approval` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.

## Cross-cutting acceptance
- No external or production action occurs.
- Independent review is completed.
- Blockers stop progression.
- Final authority remains human.
