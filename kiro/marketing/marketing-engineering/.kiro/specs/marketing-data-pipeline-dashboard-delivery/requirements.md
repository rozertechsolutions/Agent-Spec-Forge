# Marketing Data Pipeline and Dashboard Delivery — Requirements

## Objective
Execute `marketing-data-pipeline-dashboard-delivery` within the branch governance and prepare a human decision package.

## Requirements
1. The workflow shall complete the `Validate contracts, metrics, and access` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
2. The workflow shall complete the `Implement or specify ingestion and transformation` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
3. The workflow shall complete the `Add lineage and quality tests` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
4. The workflow shall complete the `Build semantic metrics and dashboard` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
5. The workflow shall complete the `Validate reconciliation, freshness, and privacy` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
6. The workflow shall complete the `Add monitoring and recovery` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
7. The workflow shall complete the `Independent QA` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
8. The workflow shall complete the `Human access/release approval` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.

## Cross-cutting acceptance
- No external or production action occurs.
- Independent review is completed.
- Blockers stop progression.
- Final authority remains human.
