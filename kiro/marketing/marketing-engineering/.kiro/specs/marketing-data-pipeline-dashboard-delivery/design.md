# Marketing Data Pipeline and Dashboard Delivery — Design

## Sequence
1. Validate contracts, metrics, and access
2. Implement or specify ingestion and transformation
3. Add lineage and quality tests
4. Build semantic metrics and dashboard
5. Validate reconciliation, freshness, and privacy
6. Add monitoring and recovery
7. Independent QA
8. Human access/release approval

## Gates
- Intake
- Evidence or technical baseline
- Compliance/security
- Independent quality
- Human decision
