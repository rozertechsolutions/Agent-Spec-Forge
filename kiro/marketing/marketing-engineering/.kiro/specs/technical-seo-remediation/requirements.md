# Technical SEO Remediation — Requirements

## Objective
Execute `technical-seo-remediation` within the branch governance and prepare a human decision package.

## Requirements
1. The workflow shall complete the `Validate site scope and indexation intent` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
2. The workflow shall complete the `Audit crawl, index, render, and performance` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
3. The workflow shall complete the `Prioritize findings` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
4. The workflow shall complete the `Implement or specify changes` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
5. The workflow shall complete the `Validate and define post-release checks` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
6. The workflow shall complete the `Prepare rollback` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
7. The workflow shall complete the `Independent QA` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
8. The workflow shall complete the `Human release approval` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.

## Cross-cutting acceptance
- No external or production action occurs.
- Independent review is completed.
- Blockers stop progression.
- Final authority remains human.
