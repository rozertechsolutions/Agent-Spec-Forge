# Technical SEO Remediation — Design

## Sequence
1. Validate site scope and indexation intent
2. Audit crawl, index, render, and performance
3. Prioritize findings
4. Implement or specify changes
5. Validate and define post-release checks
6. Prepare rollback
7. Independent QA
8. Human release approval

## Gates
- Intake
- Evidence or technical baseline
- Compliance/security
- Independent quality
- Human decision
