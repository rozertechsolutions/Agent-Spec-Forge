# Data Quality and Observability Review — Requirements

## Objective
Execute `data-quality-observability-review` within the branch governance and prepare a human decision package.

## Requirements
1. The workflow shall complete the `Identify critical data products` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
2. The workflow shall complete the `Define tests and SLOs` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
3. The workflow shall complete the `Implement or specify alerts and ownership` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
4. The workflow shall complete the `Test failure and recovery paths` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
5. The workflow shall complete the `Document incident and runbook process` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
6. The workflow shall complete the `Independent review` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.

## Cross-cutting acceptance
- No external or production action occurs.
- Independent review is completed.
- Blockers stop progression.
- Final authority remains human.
