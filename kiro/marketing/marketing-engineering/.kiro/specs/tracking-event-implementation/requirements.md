# Tracking and Event Implementation — Requirements

## Objective
Execute `tracking-event-implementation` within the branch governance and prepare a human decision package.

## Requirements
1. The workflow shall complete the `Map metrics to events` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
2. The workflow shall complete the `Define schemas and identity boundaries` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
3. The workflow shall complete the `Implement or specify instrumentation` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
4. The workflow shall complete the `Validate triggers, payloads, duplication, and consent` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
5. The workflow shall complete the `Add monitoring and rollback` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
6. The workflow shall complete the `Independent QA` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
7. The workflow shall complete the `Prepare release approval` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.

## Cross-cutting acceptance
- No external or production action occurs.
- Independent review is completed.
- Blockers stop progression.
- Final authority remains human.
