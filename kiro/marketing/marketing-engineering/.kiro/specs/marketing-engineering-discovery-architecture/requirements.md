# Marketing Engineering Discovery & Architecture — Requirements

## Objective
Execute `marketing-engineering-discovery-architecture` within the branch governance and prepare a human decision package.

## Requirements
1. The workflow shall complete the `Validate handoff` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
2. The workflow shall complete the `Inventory systems, data, environments, and owners` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
3. The workflow shall complete the `Model data flows and trust boundaries` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
4. The workflow shall complete the `Define architecture, acceptance tests, rollback, and access` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
5. The workflow shall complete the `Privacy/security review` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
6. The workflow shall complete the `Human scope approval` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.

## Cross-cutting acceptance
- No external or production action occurs.
- Independent review is completed.
- Blockers stop progression.
- Final authority remains human.
