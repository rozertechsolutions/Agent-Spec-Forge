# Landing Page and Form Delivery — Design

## Sequence
1. Validate content, design, and data purpose
2. Implement or specify accessible responsive experience
3. Implement secure form validation and minimization
4. Integrate consent-aware analytics
5. Test functionality, accessibility, performance, privacy, and security
6. Prepare deployment and rollback
7. Independent QA
8. Human release approval

## Gates
- Intake
- Evidence or technical baseline
- Compliance/security
- Independent quality
- Human decision
