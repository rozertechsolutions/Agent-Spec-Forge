# Landing Page and Form Delivery — Requirements

## Objective
Execute `landing-page-form-delivery` within the branch governance and prepare a human decision package.

## Requirements
1. The workflow shall complete the `Validate content, design, and data purpose` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
2. The workflow shall complete the `Implement or specify accessible responsive experience` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
3. The workflow shall complete the `Implement secure form validation and minimization` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
4. The workflow shall complete the `Integrate consent-aware analytics` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
5. The workflow shall complete the `Test functionality, accessibility, performance, privacy, and security` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
6. The workflow shall complete the `Prepare deployment and rollback` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
7. The workflow shall complete the `Independent QA` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
8. The workflow shall complete the `Human release approval` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.

## Cross-cutting acceptance
- No external or production action occurs.
- Independent review is completed.
- Blockers stop progression.
- Final authority remains human.
