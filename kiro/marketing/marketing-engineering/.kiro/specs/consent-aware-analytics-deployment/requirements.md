# Consent-Aware Analytics Deployment — Requirements

## Objective
Execute `consent-aware-analytics-deployment` within the branch governance and prepare a human decision package.

## Requirements
1. The workflow shall complete the `Validate approved policy inputs` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
2. The workflow shall complete the `Define consent states and regions` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
3. The workflow shall complete the `Map tags and data flows to states` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
4. The workflow shall complete the `Implement or specify fail-safe gating` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
5. The workflow shall complete the `Test grant, deny, revoke, expiry, and failures` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
6. The workflow shall complete the `Privacy/security QA` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
7. The workflow shall complete the `Prepare deployment approval` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.

## Cross-cutting acceptance
- No external or production action occurs.
- Independent review is completed.
- Blockers stop progression.
- Final authority remains human.
