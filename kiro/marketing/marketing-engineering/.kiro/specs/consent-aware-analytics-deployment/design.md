# Consent-Aware Analytics Deployment — Design

## Sequence
1. Validate approved policy inputs
2. Define consent states and regions
3. Map tags and data flows to states
4. Implement or specify fail-safe gating
5. Test grant, deny, revoke, expiry, and failures
6. Privacy/security QA
7. Prepare deployment approval

## Gates
- Intake
- Evidence or technical baseline
- Compliance/security
- Independent quality
- Human decision
