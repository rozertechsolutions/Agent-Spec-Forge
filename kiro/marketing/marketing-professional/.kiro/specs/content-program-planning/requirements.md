# Content Program Planning — Requirements

## Objective
Execute `content-program-planning` within the branch governance and prepare a human decision package.

## Requirements
1. The workflow shall complete the `Define content jobs and questions` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
2. The workflow shall complete the `Prioritize themes and formats` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
3. The workflow shall complete the `Create briefs and calendar` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
4. The workflow shall complete the `Plan distribution, reuse, maintenance, and retirement` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
5. The workflow shall complete the `Define measurement` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
6. The workflow shall complete the `Rights/accessibility/localization checks` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
7. The workflow shall complete the `Human editorial approval` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.

## Cross-cutting acceptance
- No external or production action occurs.
- Independent review is completed.
- Blockers stop progression.
- Final authority remains human.
