# Positioning & Messaging — Requirements

## Objective
Execute `positioning-messaging` within the branch governance and prepare a human decision package.

## Requirements
1. The workflow shall complete the `Validate product truth and audience evidence` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
2. The workflow shall complete the `Define category and differentiation` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
3. The workflow shall complete the `Draft value proposition` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
4. The workflow shall complete the `Build message hierarchy and proof points` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
5. The workflow shall complete the `Test objections and channel adaptability` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
6. The workflow shall complete the `Compliance review` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
7. The workflow shall complete the `Human brand/product approval` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.

## Cross-cutting acceptance
- No external or production action occurs.
- Independent review is completed.
- Blockers stop progression.
- Final authority remains human.
