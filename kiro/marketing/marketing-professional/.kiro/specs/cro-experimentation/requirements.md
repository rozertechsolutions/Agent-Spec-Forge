# CRO & Experimentation — Requirements

## Objective
Execute `cro-experimentation` within the branch governance and prepare a human decision package.

## Requirements
1. The workflow shall complete the `Validate funnel and data` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
2. The workflow shall complete the `Diagnose friction` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
3. The workflow shall complete the `Create and prioritize hypotheses` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
4. The workflow shall complete the `Define experiment, eligibility, metrics, guardrails, sample assumptions, and stop rules` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
5. The workflow shall complete the `Technical feasibility review` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
6. The workflow shall complete the `Compliance/quality review` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
7. The workflow shall complete the `Prepare implementation approval` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.

## Cross-cutting acceptance
- No external or production action occurs.
- Independent review is completed.
- Blockers stop progression.
- Final authority remains human.
