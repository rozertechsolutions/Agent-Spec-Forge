# CRO & Experimentation — Design

## Sequence
1. Validate funnel and data
2. Diagnose friction
3. Create and prioritize hypotheses
4. Define experiment, eligibility, metrics, guardrails, sample assumptions, and stop rules
5. Technical feasibility review
6. Compliance/quality review
7. Prepare implementation approval

## Gates
- Intake
- Evidence or technical baseline
- Compliance/security
- Independent quality
- Human decision
