# Social Media Planning — Requirements

## Objective
Execute `social-media-planning` within the branch governance and prepare a human decision package.

## Requirements
1. The workflow shall complete the `Validate objective, audience, and messages` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
2. The workflow shall complete the `Select channels and roles` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
3. The workflow shall complete the `Define pillars and formats` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
4. The workflow shall complete the `Build calendar and production plan` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
5. The workflow shall complete the `Define engagement, moderation, and escalation` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
6. The workflow shall complete the `Policy/accessibility/rights review` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
7. The workflow shall complete the `Prepare social approval` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.

## Cross-cutting acceptance
- No external or production action occurs.
- Independent review is completed.
- Blockers stop progression.
- Final authority remains human.
