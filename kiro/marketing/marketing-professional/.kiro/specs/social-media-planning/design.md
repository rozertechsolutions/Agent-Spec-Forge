# Social Media Planning — Design

## Sequence
1. Validate objective, audience, and messages
2. Select channels and roles
3. Define pillars and formats
4. Build calendar and production plan
5. Define engagement, moderation, and escalation
6. Policy/accessibility/rights review
7. Prepare social approval

## Gates
- Intake
- Evidence or technical baseline
- Compliance/security
- Independent quality
- Human decision
