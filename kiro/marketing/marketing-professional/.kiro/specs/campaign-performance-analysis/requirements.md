# Campaign Performance Analysis — Requirements

## Objective
Execute `campaign-performance-analysis` within the branch governance and prepare a human decision package.

## Requirements
1. The workflow shall complete the `Validate data and metric definitions` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
2. The workflow shall complete the `Reproduce KPIs` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
3. The workflow shall complete the `Compare against plan and baseline` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
4. The workflow shall complete the `Assess plausible drivers and attribution limits` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
5. The workflow shall complete the `Document uncertainty` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
6. The workflow shall complete the `Recommend actions or tests` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
7. The workflow shall complete the `Independent quality review` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.

## Cross-cutting acceptance
- No external or production action occurs.
- Independent review is completed.
- Blockers stop progression.
- Final authority remains human.
