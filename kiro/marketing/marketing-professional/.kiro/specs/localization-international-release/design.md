# Localization & International Release — Design

## Sequence
1. Confirm source intent and market availability
2. Adapt language, culture, terminology, formats, and disclosures
3. Check product, channel, and policy context
4. Qualified local review
5. Compliance review
6. Independent review
7. Prepare local release approval

## Gates
- Intake
- Evidence or technical baseline
- Compliance/security
- Independent quality
- Human decision
