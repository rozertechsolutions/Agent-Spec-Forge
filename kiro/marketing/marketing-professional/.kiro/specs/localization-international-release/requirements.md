# Localization & International Release — Requirements

## Objective
Execute `localization-international-release` within the branch governance and prepare a human decision package.

## Requirements
1. The workflow shall complete the `Confirm source intent and market availability` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
2. The workflow shall complete the `Adapt language, culture, terminology, formats, and disclosures` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
3. The workflow shall complete the `Check product, channel, and policy context` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
4. The workflow shall complete the `Qualified local review` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
5. The workflow shall complete the `Compliance review` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
6. The workflow shall complete the `Independent review` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
7. The workflow shall complete the `Prepare local release approval` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.

## Cross-cutting acceptance
- No external or production action occurs.
- Independent review is completed.
- Blockers stop progression.
- Final authority remains human.
