# Paid Media Planning & Review — Requirements

## Objective
Execute `paid-media-planning-review` within the branch governance and prepare a human decision package.

## Requirements
1. The workflow shall complete the `Validate objective, budget owner, category, claims, and destination` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
2. The workflow shall complete the `Select platforms and placements` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
3. The workflow shall complete the `Define targeting, exclusions, and creative matrix` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
4. The workflow shall complete the `Create budget scenarios` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
5. The workflow shall complete the `Define measurement and brand safety` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
6. The workflow shall complete the `Policy/compliance review` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
7. The workflow shall complete the `Prepare budget/campaign approval` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.

## Cross-cutting acceptance
- No external or production action occurs.
- Independent review is completed.
- Blockers stop progression.
- Final authority remains human.
