# Paid Media Planning & Review — Design

## Sequence
1. Validate objective, budget owner, category, claims, and destination
2. Select platforms and placements
3. Define targeting, exclusions, and creative matrix
4. Create budget scenarios
5. Define measurement and brand safety
6. Policy/compliance review
7. Prepare budget/campaign approval

## Gates
- Intake
- Evidence or technical baseline
- Compliance/security
- Independent quality
- Human decision
