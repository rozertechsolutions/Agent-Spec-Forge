# Product Launch & Go-to-Market — Design

## Sequence
1. Confirm product and market readiness
2. Define objective and audience sequence
3. Align positioning and channels
4. Plan assets, timeline, owners, and dependencies
5. Define measurement and hold criteria
6. Localization and compliance review
7. Independent review
8. Prepare launch approval

## Gates
- Intake
- Evidence or technical baseline
- Compliance/security
- Independent quality
- Human decision
