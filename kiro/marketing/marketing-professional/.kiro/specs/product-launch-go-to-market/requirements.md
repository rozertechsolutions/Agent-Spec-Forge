# Product Launch & Go-to-Market — Requirements

## Objective
Execute `product-launch-go-to-market` within the branch governance and prepare a human decision package.

## Requirements
1. The workflow shall complete the `Confirm product and market readiness` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
2. The workflow shall complete the `Define objective and audience sequence` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
3. The workflow shall complete the `Align positioning and channels` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
4. The workflow shall complete the `Plan assets, timeline, owners, and dependencies` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
5. The workflow shall complete the `Define measurement and hold criteria` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
6. The workflow shall complete the `Localization and compliance review` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
7. The workflow shall complete the `Independent review` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
8. The workflow shall complete the `Prepare launch approval` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.

## Cross-cutting acceptance
- No external or production action occurs.
- Independent review is completed.
- Blockers stop progression.
- Final authority remains human.
