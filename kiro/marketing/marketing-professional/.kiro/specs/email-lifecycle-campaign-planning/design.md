# Email & Lifecycle Campaign Planning — Design

## Sequence
1. Validate consent, sender, and suppression
2. Define eligible segments
3. Map triggers, sequence, caps, exits, and fallbacks
4. Prepare copy requirements
5. Define tests and measurement
6. Compliance review
7. Prepare CRM approval

## Gates
- Intake
- Evidence or technical baseline
- Compliance/security
- Independent quality
- Human decision
