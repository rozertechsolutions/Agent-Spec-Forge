# Email & Lifecycle Campaign Planning — Requirements

## Objective
Execute `email-lifecycle-campaign-planning` within the branch governance and prepare a human decision package.

## Requirements
1. The workflow shall complete the `Validate consent, sender, and suppression` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
2. The workflow shall complete the `Define eligible segments` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
3. The workflow shall complete the `Map triggers, sequence, caps, exits, and fallbacks` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
4. The workflow shall complete the `Prepare copy requirements` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
5. The workflow shall complete the `Define tests and measurement` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
6. The workflow shall complete the `Compliance review` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
7. The workflow shall complete the `Prepare CRM approval` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.

## Cross-cutting acceptance
- No external or production action occurs.
- Independent review is completed.
- Blockers stop progression.
- Final authority remains human.
