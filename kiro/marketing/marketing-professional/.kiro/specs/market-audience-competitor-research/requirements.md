# Market, Audience & Competitor Research — Requirements

## Objective
Execute `market-audience-competitor-research` within the branch governance and prepare a human decision package.

## Requirements
1. The workflow shall complete the `Define questions` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
2. The workflow shall complete the `Create source plan` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
3. The workflow shall complete the `Collect current evidence` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
4. The workflow shall complete the `Triangulate and normalize` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
5. The workflow shall complete the `Analyze segments and competitors` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
6. The workflow shall complete the `Document contradictions and limits` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
7. The workflow shall complete the `Independent quality review` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.

## Cross-cutting acceptance
- No external or production action occurs.
- Independent review is completed.
- Blockers stop progression.
- Final authority remains human.
