# Final Campaign Approval Preparation — Requirements

## Objective
Execute `final-campaign-approval-preparation` within the branch governance and prepare a human decision package.

## Requirements
1. The workflow shall complete the `Collect validated brief and deliverables` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
2. The workflow shall complete the `Reconcile ownership and dependencies` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
3. The workflow shall complete the `Verify evidence and measurement` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
4. The workflow shall complete the `Confirm localization and compliance resolutions` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
5. The workflow shall complete the `Run independent quality gate` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
6. The workflow shall complete the `Record residual risk and requested human decision` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.

## Cross-cutting acceptance
- No external or production action occurs.
- Independent review is completed.
- Blockers stop progression.
- Final authority remains human.
