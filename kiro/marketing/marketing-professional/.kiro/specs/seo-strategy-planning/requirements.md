# SEO Strategy & Planning — Requirements

## Objective
Execute `seo-strategy-planning` within the branch governance and prepare a human decision package.

## Requirements
1. The workflow shall complete the `Validate market, site, and search evidence` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
2. The workflow shall complete the `Model search intent` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
3. The workflow shall complete the `Create topic and content-gap map` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
4. The workflow shall complete the `Define information architecture and briefs` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
5. The workflow shall complete the `Identify technical dependencies` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
6. The workflow shall complete the `Set measurement` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
7. The workflow shall complete the `Human content/site review` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.

## Cross-cutting acceptance
- No external or production action occurs.
- Independent review is completed.
- Blockers stop progression.
- Final authority remains human.
