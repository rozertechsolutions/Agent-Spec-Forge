# Marketing Compliance Review — Design

## Sequence
1. Confirm independence and scope
2. Review privacy, consent, tracking, and targeting
3. Verify claims, disclosures, comparisons, and testimonials
4. Check rights, accessibility, policy, localization, and reputation
5. Classify findings and remediation
6. Escalate high-risk domains
7. Set review status

## Gates
- Intake
- Evidence or technical baseline
- Compliance/security
- Independent quality
- Human decision
