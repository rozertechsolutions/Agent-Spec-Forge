# Marketing Compliance Review — Requirements

## Objective
Execute `marketing-compliance-review` within the branch governance and prepare a human decision package.

## Requirements
1. The workflow shall complete the `Confirm independence and scope` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
2. The workflow shall complete the `Review privacy, consent, tracking, and targeting` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
3. The workflow shall complete the `Verify claims, disclosures, comparisons, and testimonials` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
4. The workflow shall complete the `Check rights, accessibility, policy, localization, and reputation` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
5. The workflow shall complete the `Classify findings and remediation` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
6. The workflow shall complete the `Escalate high-risk domains` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
7. The workflow shall complete the `Set review status` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.

## Cross-cutting acceptance
- No external or production action occurs.
- Independent review is completed.
- Blockers stop progression.
- Final authority remains human.
