# Marketing Copy Production & Review — Requirements

## Objective
Execute `marketing-copy-production-review` within the branch governance and prepare a human decision package.

## Requirements
1. The workflow shall complete the `Validate brief and evidence` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
2. The workflow shall complete the `Draft copy` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
3. The workflow shall complete the `Adapt to channel and locale` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
4. The workflow shall complete the `Annotate claims and disclosures` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
5. The workflow shall complete the `SEO review when relevant` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
6. The workflow shall complete the `Compliance review` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
7. The workflow shall complete the `Independent review` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
8. The workflow shall complete the `Prepare content approval` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.

## Cross-cutting acceptance
- No external or production action occurs.
- Independent review is completed.
- Blockers stop progression.
- Final authority remains human.
