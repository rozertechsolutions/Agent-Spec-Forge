# Marketing Copy Production & Review — Design

## Sequence
1. Validate brief and evidence
2. Draft copy
3. Adapt to channel and locale
4. Annotate claims and disclosures
5. SEO review when relevant
6. Compliance review
7. Independent review
8. Prepare content approval

## Gates
- Intake
- Evidence or technical baseline
- Compliance/security
- Independent quality
- Human decision
