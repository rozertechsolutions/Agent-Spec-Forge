# Marketing Strategy Development — Requirements

## Objective
Execute `marketing-strategy-development` within the branch governance and prepare a human decision package.

## Requirements
1. The workflow shall complete the `Validate brief` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
2. The workflow shall complete the `Research market, audience, and competitors` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
3. The workflow shall complete the `Define objectives and positioning` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
4. The workflow shall complete the `Select channel roles and initiatives` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
5. The workflow shall complete the `Define measurement, risk, and dependencies` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
6. The workflow shall complete the `Compliance review` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
7. The workflow shall complete the `Independent quality review` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
8. The workflow shall complete the `Prepare human approval` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.

## Cross-cutting acceptance
- No external or production action occurs.
- Independent review is completed.
- Blockers stop progression.
- Final authority remains human.
