# Integrated Campaign Planning — Requirements

## Objective
Execute `integrated-campaign-planning` within the branch governance and prepare a human decision package.

## Requirements
1. The workflow shall complete the `Validate campaign brief` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
2. The workflow shall complete the `Confirm evidence and audience` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
3. The workflow shall complete the `Define message and channel roles` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
4. The workflow shall complete the `Build asset, timing, owner, and budget-scenario matrix` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
5. The workflow shall complete the `Define measurement and contingency` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
6. The workflow shall complete the `Localization when needed` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
7. The workflow shall complete the `Compliance review` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
8. The workflow shall complete the `Independent review` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.
9. The workflow shall complete the `Prepare campaign approval` stage with owner, evidence, checks, risk, and state.

## Cross-cutting acceptance
- No external or production action occurs.
- Independent review is completed.
- Blockers stop progression.
- Final authority remains human.
