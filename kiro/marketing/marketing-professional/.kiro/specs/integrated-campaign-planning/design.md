# Integrated Campaign Planning — Design

## Sequence
1. Validate campaign brief
2. Confirm evidence and audience
3. Define message and channel roles
4. Build asset, timing, owner, and budget-scenario matrix
5. Define measurement and contingency
6. Localization when needed
7. Compliance review
8. Independent review
9. Prepare campaign approval

## Gates
- Intake
- Evidence or technical baseline
- Compliance/security
- Independent quality
- Human decision
