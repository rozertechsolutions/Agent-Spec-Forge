# Safety and Human Review

Use least privilege. Require human approval before destructive, sensitive, external, architectural, dependency, permission, trust-boundary, migration, public-contract, Git, shell, web, MCP, hook, deployment, publication, signing, release, credential, or irreversible actions. Do not expose secrets, claim unobserved validation, or activate external integrations by default.
