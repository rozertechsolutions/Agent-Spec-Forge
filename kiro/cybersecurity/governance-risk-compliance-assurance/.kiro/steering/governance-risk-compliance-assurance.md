# Governance Risk Compliance Assurance Steering

Use this only for cybersecurity GRC assurance work inside `kiro/cybersecurity/governance-risk-compliance-assurance/`. Select one primary owner for each task, keep review independent, and preserve the distinction between drafted artifacts, verified evidence, assumptions, and human approvals.

Do not use real secrets, private audit evidence, customer data, employee data, production endpoints, active MCP servers, hooks, regulator submissions, risk acceptance, compliance certification, publication, deployment, destructive operations, or financial actions.

Classify every completion criterion as `required`, `conditionally-required`, or `not-applicable` with a reason. Report unavailable evidence honestly.
