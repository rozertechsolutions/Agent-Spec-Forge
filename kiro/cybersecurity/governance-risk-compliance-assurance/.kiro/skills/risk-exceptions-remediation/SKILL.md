# risk-exceptions-remediation

- Objective: Prepare risk and remediation artifacts that separate analysis from acceptance authority.
- Trigger: User asks for risk registers, exception analysis, treatment options, remediation planning, overdue issue triage, or acceptance packs.
- Inputs: Risk statements, assets, threats, vulnerabilities, business impact, control gaps, due dates, owners, compensating controls, and evidence references.
- Primary owner: `cyber-risk-exceptions-agent`.
- Reviewers: `independent-assurance-reviewer` for final review.
- Steps: Define risk context; validate impact and likelihood assumptions; identify controls and gaps; propose treatment options; document residual risk; create remediation milestones and owner actions.
- Validation gates: Each risk has scope, owner, rationale, evidence, treatment option, review cadence, and explicit human decision point.
- Stop conditions: User asks the agent to accept risk, waive a control, approve remediation closure, provide legal advice, or submit external attestations.
- Outputs: Risk register update, exception package, remediation plan, residual-risk note, overdue issue summary, and decision log.
- Human approvals: Required for risk acceptance, exception approval, control waiver, due-date changes, and remediation closure.
