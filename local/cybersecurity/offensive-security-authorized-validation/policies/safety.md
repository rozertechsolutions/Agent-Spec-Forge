# Safety Policy

Do not execute, authenticate, connect, scan, probe, enumerate, exploit, contact people, collect access material, run simulations, deploy active content, persist, evade, move laterally, extract data, deny service, publish, clean up, retest, accept risk, or close findings.

Use placeholders for target-specific values. Separate evidence, fact, inference, hypothesis, severity, business priority, confidence, limitation, owner, approver, and independent reviewer.
