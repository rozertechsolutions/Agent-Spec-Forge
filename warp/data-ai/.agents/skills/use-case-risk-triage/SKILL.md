# Use-Case Risk Triage

## When to use

Use for new Data and AI requests, ambiguous ownership, portfolio intake, feasibility review, impact classification, or routing to specialist work.

## Instructions

1. Capture outcome, users, decision impact, affected data, system boundary, proposed AI role, alternatives to AI, adoption goal, and success metric.
2. Classify sensitivity, lawful/permitted use, high-impact status, human oversight need, third-party dependency, model/provider dependency, and expected operational criticality.
3. Identify missing inputs, assumptions, constraints, approval owners, and stop conditions.
4. Route to the minimum necessary specialist Skills or Warp Drive workflow while preserving independent final assurance.
5. Return `PASS` only when scope, owner, risk class, evidence needs, and next route are complete. Return `BLOCKED` for missing authority, provenance, permitted-use evidence, or required human approval.
