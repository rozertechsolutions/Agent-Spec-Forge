# Assurance and Independent Review Workflows

## section-assurance-review
1. Confirm reviewer independence, section scope, expected role ownership and quality gates.
2. Review static evidence and record findings with severity, owner, impact and closure criteria.

## cross-section-consistency-review
1. Check handoffs, ownership, terminology, quality gates and safety controls across sections.
2. Flag contradictions, duplicate authority, circular delegation and missing evidence.

## finding-and-remediation-cycle
1. Track finding, evidence, severity, owner, remediation plan, closure criteria and re-review trigger.
2. Block completion while critical findings remain unresolved.

## waiver-review
1. Assess waiver request, residual risk, compensating controls, expiry, approver and scope.
2. Require human risk-owner approval before accepting a waiver.

## final-operational-readiness-gate
1. Review architecture, delivery, cloud, containers, SRE, resilience, performance, DevSecOps, FinOps and sustainability evidence.
2. Return pass, conditional pass with waiver, or block decision without claiming runtime validation.
