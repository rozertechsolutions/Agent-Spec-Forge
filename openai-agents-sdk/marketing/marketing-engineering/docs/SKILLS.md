# Procedure Catalog

Authoritative catalog of procedures represented in agent instructions and workflows included in this branch.

## `marketing-engineering-intake-and-system-discovery` — Marketing Engineering Intake and System Discovery
**Owner:** Marketing Engineering Lead
Convert an approved professional handoff into a bounded technical brief, system inventory, data-flow map, ownership plan, tests, and rollback requirements.

## `tracking-plan-and-event-taxonomy-implementation` — Tracking Plan and Event Taxonomy Implementation
**Owner:** Measurement & Tracking Engineer
Implement or specify versioned event contracts, identity boundaries, consent gating, validation, monitoring, and rollback.

## `data-layer-and-tag-manager-engineering` — Data Layer and Tag Manager Engineering
**Owner:** Measurement & Tracking Engineer
Design governed data-layer contracts and tag-manager behavior with ownership, purpose, consent gating, environments, versioning, tests, and rollback.

## `conversion-api-and-ad-platform-integration` — Conversion API and Ad Platform Integration
**Owner:** Martech Integration Engineer
Design secure, consent-aware client/server conversion integrations with minimization, deduplication, observability, and human-controlled activation.

## `crm-cms-email-and-cdp-integration` — CRM, CMS, Email, and CDP Integration
**Owner:** Martech Integration Engineer
Design least-privilege, idempotent, observable integrations with explicit contracts, preference handling, deletion, testing, and recovery.

## `landing-page-and-form-engineering` — Landing Page and Form Engineering
**Owner:** Web & Landing Page Engineer
Implement or specify accessible, performant, secure, consent-aware landing pages and forms from approved content and acceptance criteria.

## `technical-seo-audit-and-remediation` — Technical SEO Audit and Remediation
**Owner:** Technical SEO Engineer
Audit and safely remediate crawl, indexation, canonicals, redirects, structured data, rendering, performance, and technical search signals.

## `consent-management-and-tag-gating` — Consent Management and Tag Gating
**Owner:** Privacy, Consent & Governance Engineer
Implement consent-state models, preference propagation, regional behavior, fail-safe tag gating, auditability, tests, and rollback.

## `experiment-instrumentation-and-feature-flags` — Experiment Instrumentation and Feature Flags
**Owner:** Experimentation & CRO Engineer
Implement assignment, exposure logging, flags, metrics, integrity checks, guardrails, monitoring, kill switches, and rollback from an approved contract.

## `marketing-data-pipeline-and-transformation` — Marketing Data Pipeline and Transformation
**Owner:** Marketing Data & Analytics Engineer
Design governed ingestion and transformation pipelines with contracts, lineage, quality, privacy, recovery, and change control.

## `dashboard-and-semantic-metric-engineering` — Dashboard and Semantic Metric Engineering
**Owner:** Marketing Data & Analytics Engineer
Implement consistent semantic metrics and decision-focused dashboards with ownership, lineage, access, freshness, filters, and validation.

## `marketing-data-quality-and-observability` — Marketing Data Quality and Observability
**Owner:** Marketing Data & Analytics Engineer
Define quality rules, service indicators, alerts, ownership, incident handling, recovery, and evidence for marketing data products.

## `accessibility-performance-and-web-quality` — Accessibility, Performance, and Web Quality
**Owner:** Web & Landing Page Engineer
Validate accessible interaction, performance, resilience, privacy behavior, security basics, and user-visible quality before release.

## `integration-security-and-secret-handling` — Integration Security and Secret Handling
**Owner:** Marketing Engineering Security & QA Reviewer
Review least privilege, external secret management, validation, transport security, logging minimization, threat controls, and test evidence.

## `release-rollback-and-change-validation` — Release, Rollback, and Change Validation
**Owner:** Marketing Engineering Lead
Prepare a controlled release package with approvals, monitoring, rollback, migration safety, post-release validation, and human execution.

## `marketing-engineering-final-quality-gate` — Marketing Engineering Final Quality Gate
**Owner:** Marketing Engineering Security & QA Reviewer
Independently verify scope, contracts, tests, security, privacy, accessibility, observability, rollback, and readiness for a human release decision.
