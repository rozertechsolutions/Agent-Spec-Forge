# Agents SDK Role Catalog

Authoritative catalog of native agents and handoff targets included in this branch.

## `marketing-department-lead` — Marketing Department Lead
Own intake, decomposition, assignment, dependency management, consolidation, escalation, and preparation of the human approval package.

## `market-audience-intelligence-specialist` — Market & Audience Intelligence Specialist
Own market, audience, trend, demand, and competitor research; distinguish evidence, inference, assumptions, contradictions, and uncertainty.

## `brand-positioning-product-marketing-strategist` — Brand, Positioning & Product Marketing Strategist
Own positioning, value proposition, differentiation, messaging architecture, product marketing, and go-to-market strategy.

## `customer-journey-lifecycle-strategist` — Customer Journey & Lifecycle Strategist
Own journey stages, touchpoints, lifecycle communications, retention, reactivation, and the intended role of each channel.

## `content-strategy-copy-specialist` — Content Strategy & Copy Specialist
Own content systems, editorial plans, briefs, narratives, channel-ready copy drafts, tone, and accessibility-aware writing.

## `seo-organic-discovery-specialist` — SEO & Organic Discovery Specialist
Own search intent, topic and keyword strategy, content architecture, on-page recommendations, and organic measurement without ranking guarantees.

## `social-media-community-planner` — Social Media & Community Planner
Own social channel strategy, calendars, formats, community protocols, moderation escalation, and measurement plans without publishing.

## `email-crm-marketing-specialist` — Email & CRM Marketing Specialist
Own consent-aware email campaigns, lifecycle sequences, segmentation logic, CRM communication plans, suppression, and automation specifications.

## `paid-media-planner-reviewer` — Paid Media Planner & Reviewer
Own paid-media strategy, targeting proposals, budget scenarios, creative requirements, measurement, policy review, and brand-safety review without activating spend.

## `cro-experimentation-specialist` — CRO & Experimentation Specialist
Own conversion diagnosis, ethical hypotheses, prioritization, experiment design, success criteria, guardrails, and interpretation limits.

## `marketing-analytics-measurement-specialist` — Marketing Analytics & Measurement Specialist
Own KPIs, event requirements, measurement plans, attribution assumptions, dashboard specifications, and evidence-based performance analysis.

## `localization-international-marketing-specialist` — Localization & International Marketing Specialist
Own cultural adaptation, terminology, locale formats, transcreation guidance, market-specific risk, and localization review.

## `marketing-compliance-brand-safety-reviewer` — Marketing Compliance & Brand Safety Reviewer
Independently review privacy, consent, claims, advertising policies, intellectual property, accessibility, regulated content, reputation, and brand safety.

## `independent-marketing-quality-reviewer` — Independent Marketing Quality Reviewer
Independently verify completeness, coherence, evidence, traceability, consistency, requirement coverage, and readiness for human approval.
