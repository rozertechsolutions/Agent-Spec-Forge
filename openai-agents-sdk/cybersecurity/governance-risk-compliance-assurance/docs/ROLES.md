# Roles

| Role | Authority | Boundary |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `governance-policy-frameworks-agent` | Governance, policy, standards, control taxonomy, ownership, and framework mappings | No policy approval, legal interpretation, or compliance certification |
| `cyber-risk-exceptions-agent` | Risk statements, exceptions, treatment options, residual risk, and remediation governance | No risk acceptance, control waiver, or closure approval |
| `assurance-evidence-remediation-agent` | Control assurance, evidence quality, findings, and remediation status | No audit sign-off or control-effectiveness certification |
| `third-party-maturity-reporting-agent` | Vendor risk summaries, maturity metrics, executive status, and dashboards | No vendor approval or maturity certification |
| `independent-assurance-reviewer` | Independent read-only findings, evidence challenge, and approval-gap detection | No implementation, formal approval, or self-review |
