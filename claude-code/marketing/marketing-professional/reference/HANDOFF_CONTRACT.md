# Professional-to-Engineering Handoff Contract

## Purpose
Transfer an approved marketing requirement into a bounded technical request without moving business decision authority into Marketing Engineering.

## Required fields
- Professional owner and engineering owner.
- Approved objective, audience, journey stage, channel, market, and success metric.
- Exact implementation scope and explicit exclusions.
- Claims, content, localization, accessibility, privacy, consent, retention, and policy requirements.
- Tracking taxonomy, data classification, allowed destinations, and prohibited data.
- Systems, interfaces, environments, dependencies, and compatibility constraints.
- Acceptance tests, monitoring, release strategy, rollback conditions, reviewers, and approval authority.

## Rules
- Marketing Professional owns intent, claims, audience rationale, channel plan, and business acceptance.
- Marketing Engineering owns technical design, implementation evidence, security, testing, observability, deployment preparation, and rollback.
- Neither branch may silently change the other's approved decisions.
- Contradictory or incomplete fields block implementation.
- Handoff completion never authorizes a production release or live campaign action.
