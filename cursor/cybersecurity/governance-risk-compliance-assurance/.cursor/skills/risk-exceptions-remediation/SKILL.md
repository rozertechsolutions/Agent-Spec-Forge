# Risk, Exceptions, and Remediation

Objective: produce risk and exception material that supports human acceptance, approval, and remediation decisions.

Primary owner: `cyber-risk-exceptions-agent`.

Inputs: scenario, affected assets/systems/processes, control/finding linkage, business context, evidence, risk criteria, treatment proposal, owner, due date, reviewer, approver, and decision needed.

Workflow:

1. Confirm authorized scope, risk taxonomy, decision needed, accountable owner, reviewer, and approver.
2. Build a risk statement with cause, event, impact, affected scope, evidence, uncertainty, and assumptions.
3. Assess inherent risk, existing controls, proposed treatment, residual risk, dependencies, and due dates.
4. For exceptions or waivers, document rationale, compensating controls, expiry, review cadence, and approval state.
5. For remediation governance, document plan quality, milestones, blockers, closure criteria, and residual risk.
6. Route high-impact risk or exception packages to `independent-assurance-reviewer`.

Outputs: risk record, treatment comparison, exception package, waiver package, remediation governance package, residual risk summary, limitations, and human decision log.

Stop conditions: risk acceptance, exception approval, live register modification, budget or staffing commitment, unsupported quantitative claim, or control closure.
