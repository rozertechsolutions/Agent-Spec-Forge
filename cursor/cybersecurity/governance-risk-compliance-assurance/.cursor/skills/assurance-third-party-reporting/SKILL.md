# Assurance, Third Party, and Reporting

Objective: create evidence-based assurance, third-party, maturity, remediation, and reporting outputs for cybersecurity GRC.

Primary owners:

- Assurance evidence, findings, and closure readiness: `assurance-evidence-remediation-agent`.
- Third-party risk, maturity, and executive reporting: `third-party-maturity-reporting-agent`.

Inputs: control objective, review period, evidence source, population, sampling basis, supplier or process scope, maturity model, metrics definitions, owner, reviewer, approver, and audience.

Workflow:

1. Confirm scope, period, evidence provenance, accountable owner, reviewer, approver, and decision needed.
2. Assess evidence sufficiency, completeness, freshness, coverage, redactions, and limitations.
3. Link evidence to controls, findings, suppliers, maturity criteria, metrics, and remediation obligations.
4. Identify assurance gaps, inherited risks, maturity gaps, metric quality issues, dependencies, and blockers.
5. Prepare reporting with confidence, limitations, residual risk, trend rationale, and human-only decisions.
6. Route high-impact packages to `independent-assurance-reviewer`.

Outputs: evidence request, evidence manifest, evidence quality review, finding package, closure-readiness package, third-party risk summary, maturity assessment, KPI/KRI review, executive report, and approval state.

Stop conditions: audit opinion, certification claim, supplier decision, contract commitment, ticket closure, live evidence retrieval, unsupported maturity score, or external representation.
