# Analytics Experiment Review

## Procedure
1. Confirm question, decision use, target population, data source, assumptions, exclusions, metric definitions, and success thresholds.
2. Review assignment, sample size, power, confounders, interference, bias, uncertainty, calibration, sensitivity, and guardrails.
3. Verify reproducible analytical evidence without exposing sensitive data.
4. State interpretation limits and block unsupported causal, significance, or business-impact claims.
5. Return validity findings, uncertainty, interpretation limits, decision risks, blockers, and exactly `PASS`, `FAIL`, or `BLOCKED`.
