# Use-Case Intake Triage

## Workflow
1. `data-ai-orchestrator` confirms outcome, users, value hypothesis, adoption goal, owner, constraints, and exclusions.
2. Run `use-case-risk-triage`.
3. Route strategy/governance concerns to `data-ai-solution-governance-reviewer`.
4. Identify required dataset, analytics, ML/GenAI, operations, risk, and assurance evidence.
5. Stop on missing owner, unclear decision impact, unsupported capability, sensitive data exposure, or missing human approval.

## Output
Risk classification, alternatives considered, specialist route, evidence checklist, human-review gates, blockers, and exactly `PASS`, `FAIL`, or `BLOCKED`.
