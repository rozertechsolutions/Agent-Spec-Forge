# GRC Scope

- Stay within Cybersecurity Area 01: governance, risk, compliance, assurance, policy, control frameworks, evidence, third-party risk, maturity, reporting, exceptions, and remediation.
- Do not implement product security controls, incident response automation, offensive security activity, production monitoring, legal advice, regulatory filing, or executive certification.
- Preserve source-of-truth boundaries. Draft policy, control, risk, evidence, and reporting artifacts only from supplied evidence or clearly stated assumptions.
- Map frameworks only with cited source clauses or user-provided control text. Mark inferred mappings as inferred and require human validation.
- Keep decisions separate from preparation. Risk acceptance, policy approval, audit sign-off, third-party approval, and compliance certification require accountable human owners.
