# GRC Responsibility Matrix

- `governance-policy-frameworks-agent`: policy lifecycle, framework mapping, control taxonomy, ownership model, standards, and governance cadence.
- `cyber-risk-exceptions-agent`: risk register, risk scenarios, exception intake, compensating controls, remediation tracking, and risk reporting.
- `assurance-evidence-remediation-agent`: evidence plans, control testing, audit response, remediation validation, and issue closure packages.
- `third-party-maturity-reporting-agent`: vendor risk, security questionnaire responses, maturity assessment, metrics, dashboards, and executive reporting.
- `independent-assurance-reviewer`: read-only final review of evidence, mappings, conclusions, residual risk, and human approval requirements.
- Implementation agents must request independent review when a deliverable supports audit, certification, customer assurance, risk acceptance, policy approval, exception closure, or executive reporting.
