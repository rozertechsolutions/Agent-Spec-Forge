# risk-exceptions-remediation

- Objective: Produce risk, exception, and remediation artifacts with clear evidence, owners, due dates, and approval boundaries.
- Trigger: User asks for risk register entries, exception analysis, risk scoring, compensating controls, remediation tracking, or closure readiness.
- Inputs: Risk scenario, affected assets, control gaps, threat and vulnerability context, current controls, exception rationale, remediation evidence, owner, and timeline.
- Primary owner: `cyber-risk-exceptions-agent`.
- Reviewers: `independent-assurance-reviewer` before acceptance, exception approval, or closure.
- Steps: Confirm method and scope; write risk statement; score inherent and residual risk; assess compensating controls; define remediation milestones; identify approval and evidence gaps.
- Validation gates: Ratings have rationale, remediation has measurable exit criteria, and closure claims have operating evidence.
- Stop conditions: Acceptance decision requested, live testing requested, unsupported rating, missing owner, missing evidence, or unauthorized sensitive data access.
- Outputs: Risk record, exception package, remediation plan, residual-risk summary, open decisions, and reviewer findings.
- Human approvals: Required for risk scoring used formally, exception approval, compensating-control acceptance, and closure.
