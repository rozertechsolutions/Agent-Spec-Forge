# governance-policy-frameworks

- Objective: Produce governance and framework artifacts that are traceable, scoped, and ready for human review.
- Trigger: User asks for policy drafting, control cataloging, framework mapping, standards alignment, governance cadence, ownership, or compliance crosswalks.
- Inputs: Scope, business context, policy text, framework requirements, control catalog, existing procedures, owner list, and evidence references.
- Primary owner: `governance-policy-frameworks-agent`.
- Reviewers: `independent-assurance-reviewer` for final review.
- Steps: Confirm scope; identify source authorities; map requirements to controls; separate direct evidence from inference; draft ownership and governance cadence; list gaps and decisions.
- Validation gates: Every mapping has a source, owner, applicability note, and human validation flag when inferred.
- Stop conditions: Legal interpretation, unsupported compliance claim, missing authoritative source, customer-facing claim, regulator-facing claim, or approval request.
- Outputs: Policy or standard draft, control crosswalk, RACI, gap register, decision log, and reviewer findings.
- Human approvals: Required for policy approval, compliance claims, ownership changes, framework applicability, and external distribution.
