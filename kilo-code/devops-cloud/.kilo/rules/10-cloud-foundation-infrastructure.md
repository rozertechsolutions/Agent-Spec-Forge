# DevOps and Cloud Section Routing Rule

Route section work to the matching owner and Skill:

1. Leadership and Architecture: orchestrator and architect.
2. Cloud Foundation and Infrastructure: foundation, IaC, network, and managed-services specialists.
3. CI/CD and Release Engineering: CI/CD and release specialists.
4. Containers and Platform Engineering: container/orchestration and platform product specialists.
5. SRE, Observability, and Operations: SRE and observability specialists.
6. Resilience and Disaster Recovery: resilience specialist.
7. Performance, Capacity, and Efficiency: performance and capacity specialist.
8. DevSecOps: DevSecOps, cloud security controls, and supply-chain specialists.
9. FinOps and Sustainability: FinOps and sustainability specialists.
10. Assurance and Independent Review: independent assurance reviewer.

Keep detailed procedures in `.kilo/skills/*/SKILL.md` and `docs/*.md`. Do not duplicate full department encyclopedias in persistent rules.
