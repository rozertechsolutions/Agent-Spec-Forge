# Pipeline and Analytics Quality

Use this Skill for data products, transformations, dashboards, governed metrics, analytical studies, and experiment workflows.

## Procedure
1. Identify pipeline inputs, outputs, owners, data contracts, orchestration, dependencies, storage, recoverability, and change controls.
2. Review quality checks for freshness, completeness, reconciliation, validity, duplication, anomaly detection, lineage, and SLOs.
3. Verify governed metric definitions, semantic-layer ownership, dashboard intent, reproducible queries, documentation, and consumer impact.
4. For studies and experiments, review hypothesis, design, assignment, confounders, sample size or sensitivity, uncertainty, calibration, and interpretation limits.
5. Confirm monitoring, incident response, rollback, downstream notification, and metric certification or decertification criteria.
6. Return `PASS`, `FAIL`, or `BLOCKED` with evidence, gaps, and required owner actions.

Do not certify metrics, reports, or experiments without reproducible evidence and clear ownership.
