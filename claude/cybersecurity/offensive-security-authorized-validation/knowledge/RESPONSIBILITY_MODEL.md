# Responsibility Model

Mandatory domains: authorization and rules of engagement; penetration-test planning; adversary emulation and red-team design; purple-team and control-validation coordination; social-engineering governance; evidence and finding quality; operational safety, deconfliction, and cleanup assurance; breach and attack simulation governance; retest and remediation validation; independent offensive assurance.

Area 07 does not own risk acceptance, production administration, vulnerability program ownership, SOC operations, incident command, legal authority, or unrestricted research.

Independence rule: high-impact tests, findings, cleanup claims, retest conclusions, report release, and closure recommendations require review by an owner who did not create the artifact.
