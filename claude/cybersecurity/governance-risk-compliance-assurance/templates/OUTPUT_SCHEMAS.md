## Required deliverables

- Governance charter: decision rights, accountability model, governance cadence, escalation paths, unresolved decisions.
- Responsibility matrix: role, authority, consultation, approval, evidence, conflict rule.
- Policy lifecycle record: policy hierarchy, mandatory statements, review trigger, exception path, traceability.
- Control library entry: objective, requirement source, control design, operating expectation, evidence, owner, reviewer.
- Requirement-to-control mapping: source requirement, version, mapping type, rationale, confidence, gap, remediation.
- Cyber risk statement: cause, event, impact, assets, existing controls, inherent risk, residual risk, uncertainty, treatment options.
- Risk treatment plan: treatment type, actions, dependencies, owner, target date, evidence, residual risk, approval needed.
- Exception or waiver record: requirement, scope, rationale, duration, compensating controls, monitoring, expiry, approver.
- Evidence manifest: control, period, population, source, custody, freshness, completeness, sensitivity, gaps, validation result.
- Control-effectiveness assessment: design effectiveness, operating effectiveness, evidence basis, exceptions, limitations.
- Third-party assessment: service, data, access, dependency, attestation, inherited/shared/retained risks, findings, conditions.
- Finding and remediation record: source, severity, owner, due date, root cause, remediation, validation criteria, closure evidence.
- Maturity assessment: model/version, scope, criteria, evidence, capability rating, gaps, confidence, improvement plan.
- KPI/KRI definition: metric purpose, formula, source, period, owner, quality checks, threshold basis, limitations.
- Executive report: decisions needed, material risks, control performance, findings, exceptions, trends, assumptions, limitations.
- Regulatory-change impact assessment: authoritative source, version delta, affected controls/policies/evidence, uncertainty, review plan.
- Independent assurance review record: artifact reviewed, reviewer independence, tests performed, findings, required corrections, decision readiness.
