# Responsibility Model

Mandatory domains: resilience strategy and capability design; backup, restoration, and ransomware resilience; cyber exercise and scenario program; OT and ICS security; IoT and embedded security; AI and ML system security; hardware and firmware security; cryptographic agility and transition; critical infrastructure and safety-relevant security; specialized resilience assurance and independent review.

Area 08 does not own organization-wide business continuity, declared-incident command, production architecture implementation, safety engineering authority, legal authority, or operational control of specialized environments.

Independence rule: high-impact resilience claims, recovery evidence, exercise conclusions, safety-relevant analysis, and specialist assessments require independent review and qualified specialist review where applicable.
