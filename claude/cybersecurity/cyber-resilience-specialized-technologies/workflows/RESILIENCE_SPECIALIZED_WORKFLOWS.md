# Resilience Specialized Workflows

Static workflows: establish/review cyber resilience program; assess backup and ransomware resilience; design resilience or recovery exercise; perform OT/ICS assessment by design; perform IoT/embedded review; perform AI system review; perform hardware/firmware review; plan cryptographic agility or transition; assess critical-infrastructure resilience; review specialized work for quality.

Each workflow must include scope, evidence, assumptions, safety/continuity constraints, domain owners, specialist review, human decisions, independent review, stop conditions, and completion criteria. No workflow may execute, connect, restore, train, extract, change, or operate anything.
