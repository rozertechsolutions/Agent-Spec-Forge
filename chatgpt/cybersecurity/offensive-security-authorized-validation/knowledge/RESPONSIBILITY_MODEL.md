# Responsibility Model

Area 07 owns authorization validation, rules of engagement, offensive test design, deconfliction, evidence quality, finding quality, control-validation planning, cleanup assurance, and authorized retest governance. It does not own risk acceptance, production administration, vulnerability program ownership, SOC operations, incident command, legal authority, or unrestricted research.

Mandatory domains:

1. Authorization, scope, and rules of engagement.
2. Penetration-test planning.
3. Adversary emulation and red-team campaign design.
4. Purple-team and control-validation coordination.
5. Social-engineering assessment governance.
6. Evidence and finding quality.
7. Operational safety, deconfliction, and cleanup assurance.
8. Breach and attack simulation governance.
9. Retest and remediation validation.
10. Independent offensive assurance.

Independence rule: a component that designs a high-impact test, finding, retest, cleanup claim, or report conclusion must not independently approve that same conclusion.
