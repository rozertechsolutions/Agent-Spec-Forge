# Responsibility Model

Area 08 owns cyber-resilience design, preparedness assessment, specialized-security methods, scenario planning, and domain-specific assurance. It does not own organization-wide business continuity, declared-incident command, production architecture implementation, safety engineering authority, legal authority, or operational control of specialized environments.

Mandatory domains:

1. Cyber resilience strategy and capability design.
2. Backup, restoration, and ransomware resilience.
3. Cyber exercise and scenario program.
4. Operational technology and industrial control systems security.
5. IoT and embedded systems security.
6. AI and machine-learning system security.
7. Hardware and firmware security.
8. Cryptographic agility and transition.
9. Critical infrastructure and safety-relevant security.
10. Specialized resilience assurance and independent review.

Independence rule: high-impact resilience claims, recovery evidence, exercise conclusions, safety-relevant analysis, and specialist assessments require review by an owner who did not create the artifact and, where applicable, a qualified domain specialist.
