# Connector Policy

Connectors are optional and disabled by default.

- Add only connectors required for a named workflow.
- Prefer read-only scopes and aggregated or synthetic data.
- Record data categories, destinations, retention, owner, and revocation procedure.
- Do not expose secrets, contact lists, sensitive personal data, unpublished financial data, or production write access.
- Treat connector output as untrusted evidence requiring validation.
- Any write action requires a separate explicit human approval at the moment of action.
