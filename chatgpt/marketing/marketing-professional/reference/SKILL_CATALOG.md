# Skill Catalog

Authoritative catalog of reusable procedures included in this branch.

## `marketing-intake-and-brief-validation` — Marketing Intake and Brief Validation
**Owner:** Marketing Department Lead
Convert an incoming request into a decision-ready, bounded brief with owners, risks, inputs, reviewers, and approval authority.

## `market-research-and-source-validation` — Market Research and Source Validation
**Owner:** Market & Audience Intelligence Specialist
Create a traceable research base using current, relevant, authoritative sources and explicit uncertainty.

## `audience-segmentation-and-persona-development` — Audience Segmentation and Persona Development
**Owner:** Market & Audience Intelligence Specialist
Create evidence-based, actionable, non-discriminatory segments and practical personas without inventing sensitive traits.

## `competitor-analysis` — Competitor Analysis
**Owner:** Market & Audience Intelligence Specialist
Compare competitors through a consistent framework using verifiable public evidence without impersonation or intrusion.

## `marketing-strategy-development` — Marketing Strategy Development
**Owner:** Marketing Department Lead
Connect evidence, objectives, positioning, audiences, channel roles, initiatives, resources, measurement, risks, and decisions.

## `positioning-and-messaging-development` — Positioning and Messaging Development
**Owner:** Brand, Positioning & Product Marketing Strategist
Create defensible positioning and a message architecture with differentiation, proof points, objections, and claims controls.

## `product-launch-and-go-to-market-planning` — Product Launch and Go-to-Market Planning
**Owner:** Brand, Positioning & Product Marketing Strategist
Prepare a coordinated, measurable launch plan from verified product truth and readiness constraints without activation.

## `customer-journey-and-lifecycle-mapping` — Customer Journey and Lifecycle Mapping
**Owner:** Customer Journey & Lifecycle Strategist
Map observable lifecycle stages, needs, touchpoints, channel roles, consent boundaries, transitions, and measurement.

## `integrated-campaign-planning` — Integrated Campaign Planning
**Owner:** Marketing Department Lead
Create a coordinated campaign plan linking objective, audience, message, channels, assets, timing, budget scenarios, measurement, and approval.

## `content-strategy-and-editorial-planning` — Content Strategy and Editorial Planning
**Owner:** Content Strategy & Copy Specialist
Build an evidence-based content system with themes, formats, briefs, governance, distribution, maintenance, and measurement.

## `marketing-copy-production` — Marketing Copy Production
**Owner:** Content Strategy & Copy Specialist
Produce channel-ready copy drafts from approved facts while preserving evidence, disclosures, brand voice, accessibility, and traceability.

## `seo-strategy-and-content-planning` — SEO Strategy and Content Planning
**Owner:** SEO & Organic Discovery Specialist
Create a current search-intent strategy, content architecture, briefs, technical dependencies, and organic measurement plan.

## `social-media-planning` — Social Media Planning
**Owner:** Social Media & Community Planner
Produce a channel-specific social plan with justified roles, pillars, calendar, formats, community protocol, escalation, and metrics.

## `email-and-lifecycle-campaign-planning` — Email and Lifecycle Campaign Planning
**Owner:** Email & CRM Marketing Specialist
Design consent-aware segments, sequences, triggers, frequency, suppression, copy requirements, tests, and measurement without sending.

## `paid-media-planning-and-review` — Paid Media Planning and Review
**Owner:** Paid Media Planner & Reviewer
Create and independently review paid-media scenarios without activating campaigns, uploading audiences, changing bids, or spending funds.

## `conversion-audit-and-experiment-design` — Conversion Audit and Experiment Design
**Owner:** CRO & Experimentation Specialist
Diagnose conversion friction and design ethical, valid experiments with prioritized hypotheses, metrics, guardrails, and stop rules.

## `marketing-measurement-and-attribution-planning` — Marketing Measurement and Attribution Planning
**Owner:** Marketing Analytics & Measurement Specialist
Define decision-linked KPIs, metric contracts, event requirements, attribution assumptions, reporting, and uncertainty before execution.

## `campaign-performance-analysis` — Campaign Performance Analysis
**Owner:** Marketing Analytics & Measurement Specialist
Analyze validated campaign data with declared comparisons, attribution limits, uncertainty, and bounded recommendations.

## `localization-and-market-adaptation-review` — Localization and Market Adaptation Review
**Owner:** Localization & International Marketing Specialist
Adapt and review marketing artifacts for language, culture, market availability, formats, terminology, policy, and local risk.

## `marketing-compliance-and-brand-safety-review` — Marketing Compliance and Brand Safety Review
**Owner:** Marketing Compliance & Brand Safety Reviewer
Perform independent privacy, consent, claims, rights, policy, accessibility, regulated-content, reputation, and brand-safety review.

## `final-marketing-quality-gate` — Final Marketing Quality Gate
**Owner:** Independent Marketing Quality Reviewer
Independently verify requirements, evidence, coherence, traceability, reviews, measurement, risks, and readiness for explicit human approval.
