# Apps and Connectors Policy

This file is manual policy source only. It does not configure, enable, authenticate, or activate any ChatGPT app or connector.

## Default State

No app or connector is selected. No endpoint, account identifier, credential, token, private URL, or authentication step belongs in this repository package.

## Use Conditions

A human must select, authorize, scope, and review every app or connector for a named task. Use the least data scope, require confirmation before write actions, prohibit credential copying into prompts or files, and record any data that leaves the workspace in the conversation evidence.

Follow `project/knowledge/software-development-safety-policy.md` for sensitive and external actions.
